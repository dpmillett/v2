Services

Rita’s Place, a welcoming and charming Italian restaurant, opened in 1959. From the beginning, owners Rita and Charlie Rossi worked tirelessly, preparing every dish from scratch and using only the freshest seasonal ingredients.

Within a few years, Rita’s became one of the most beloved restaurants and a local landmark praised for its mouth-watering nine-course dinners, outstanding customer service and exceptional attention to detail.

Rita’s has continued a longstanding tradition of excellence. Responding to request from customers who were interested in savoring our delicious recipes in their own setting, we established Rita’s Catering with a mission to serve restaurant quality cuisine to corporate and private clients throughout the Boston area.

We have the passion to entertain and have since become one of the most cherished caterers and partners. From table design, flower arrangements, music and much more, we are the helping hand in planning your special day!
